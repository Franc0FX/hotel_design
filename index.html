Aria Suites and Spa

Seascapes Resort Town, Jeju Island, Asaba Delta State.

Greetings and welcome to our hotel! We are delighted to offer you a luxurious and relaxing stay in our beautiful and comfortable accomodations. Our spacious rooms are equipped with all the amnities you need to fel at home, and our friendly and professional staff is always here to help you with anything you need.

Our hotel is located in the heart of the city, close to all best attractions and restaurants.

The car parking and the Wi-Fi are always free, so you can stay in touch and come and go as you please. Conveniently situated in the Jeju Island part of Asaba, this property puts you close to attractions and interesting dining options. Don't leave before paying a visit to the famous Park Vega Waterpark. Rated with 5 stars, this high-quality property provides guests with access to massage, bar, gym, cinema, restaurant and hot tub on-site.
